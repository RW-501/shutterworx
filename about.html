Who We Are

ShutterWorx is a photography platform dedicated to empowering photographers to manage, protect, and monetize their event photos with ease. Our mission is to provide photographers with the tools they need to thrive in the digital age, including custom watermarking, private galleries, and seamless photo sales.

Our Services

  • Upload & Watermark Photos: Custom watermarking and organization by event.
  • Event Galleries: Secure, private galleries with unique access codes for your clients.
  • Sell Photos: Easily sell photos through integrated payment systems like Stripe, with detailed earnings tracking.
