Welcome to ShutterWorx

The ShutterWorx platform helps photographers easily manage and monetize their event photos. Whether you're capturing memories or working on professional shoots, we've got the tools you need to succeed.

Who We Are

ShutterWorx is a photography platform dedicated to empowering photographers to manage, protect, and monetize their event photos with ease. Our mission is to provide photographers with the tools they need to thrive in the digital age, including:

  • Custom watermarking
  • Private, secure galleries
  • Seamless photo sales and earnings tracking

How ShutterWorx Works

  1. Upload & Watermark Photos: Easily apply custom watermarks and organize photos by event.
  2. Event Galleries: Create secure, private galleries accessible only via custom access codes.
  3. Sell Photos: Integrate with Stripe to sell photos online and track your earnings in detail.
